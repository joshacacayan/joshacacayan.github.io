Music and Mental Health

Presentation

Music Results

For this data science study, I ran a prediction model based on how music can impact mental health. The primary question I wanted to find out was what factors contribute to how music impacts a listeners mental health.
The dataset used for this study is known as Music and Mental Health Survey Results found on kaggle.com.
While not programming based as the application used for data analysis is weka, it helped me understand important data science classification models such as AVL tree, decision tree, KNN, and Bayes theorem.
